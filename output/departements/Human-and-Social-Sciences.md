Human and Social Sciences
Humanities and communication

#### AIMS

The aim of this course is to give students an introduction to human sciences. Groups of  five
students have to give a conference on a question which is relevant to human sciences. This
conference has to be creative.

#### CONTENT

This course gives a short overview of the history of human sciences from the eighteenth
century  towards nowaday. It also emphasizes the originality of  human sciences which try to
give critical  interpretations of the human social and cultural productions.
After this short introduction, groups of five students have to choose a theme which is relevant
to human sciences in order to give a creative conference. They are helped by their professor
to define their working theme.
After a literature search, each group defines a correct problematics of its working theme and
gives one hour conference which has to be creative (using theatrical devices, audiovisual
resources¿)
Each conference is followed by a debate  with the other students.
